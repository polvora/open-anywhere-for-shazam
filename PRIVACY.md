# Privacy Policy

**Open Anywhere for Shazam** does not collect, store, transmit, or share any
personal data.

- No analytics, no tracking, no telemetry.
- No network requests are made by the extension itself.
- No data leaves your browser.

The extension only runs on `shazam.com` song pages. It reads the song title and
artist already shown on the page and builds standard search links for YouTube,
YouTube Music and Spotify. Those links open only when **you** click them, in a
new tab, exactly as if you had typed the search yourself.

No permissions beyond running a content script on `shazam.com` are requested.
